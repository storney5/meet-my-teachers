Course Teacher

Dr. Syrenthia Robinson

Course Description

The objective of this course is to introduce basic tools of multimedia production. In this class, I’ve been able to learn how to use Adobe Creative Cloud software applications like Audition, Premiere Pro and Illustrator. I’ve been able to create a radio commercial and my own sound effect in Audition, a B-roll video of Torg Bridge and a how-to video in Premiere Pro and recreate the VT logos and come up with my own in Illustrator. In lectures, we’ve learned about the early stages of digital media and how each medium got to the stage that it is now in 2018. We’ve also learned about different techniques that come with digital media technology.

Teacher's Biography

Dr. Robinson has her doctorate in communication and information from the University of Tennessee. Prior to that degree, Dr. Robinson got her master’s in mass communication and her bachelor’s in radio, television, film/broadcast journalism from the University of Southern Mississippi. She has professional experience in the field working as a production manager for HGTV and news producer for news networks in Knoxville, Tennessee, and Hattiesburg, Mississippi. Dr. Robinson won the 1st Place Debut Paper Award in the Courses Curricula Division at the 52nd Annual Broadcast Education Association Convention, as well as the Best Research Award at the 23rd Annual College of Communications Research Symposium at the University of Tennessee. Dr. Robinson has been nothing but helpful and a great teacher for me this semester.

What Do I Think of This Course?

I like this course. I took it as one of my major electives and I’m very happy I did. The experience I’m getting with different Adobe Creative Cloud applications is really helpful for me since I envision myself using these programs in my career. I’ve enjoyed all the projects we’ve had to do, and the class lectures are interesting. We’ve also had the chance to go into the studio in Moss Arts Center and learn about and practice the different roles that go into producing a news show.
