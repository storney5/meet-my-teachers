Course Teacher

Dr. Carlos Evia

Course Description

In Visual Media, we learn the how to create media in visual form. Like Media Production Technology, we use Adobe Creative Cloud programs such as InDesign, Photoshop and Premiere Pro. We’ve learned how to create effective multimedia like a magazine pilot with InDesign, a photo gallery using DSLR cameras, a video with Premiere Pro and now a website using tools like Atom and GitHub.

Teacher's Biography

Dr. Evia earned his doctorate in technical communication and rhetoric from Texas Tech University. Prior to that degree, Dr. Evia earned his bachelor’s in communication science from Instituto de Ciencias Sociales de Mérida in Yucatan, Mexico. He also earned his master’s in computer systems from Universidad La Salle in Mexico City. Dr. Evia has written books and contributed to others related to communication. He has also been featured in numerous scholarly journal articles. He has won both the Faculty Inclusiveness and Innovation award and the Outstanding Faculty Outreach award from Virginia Tech as well as the Frank R. Smith Award for Distinguished Article in the Journal Technical Communication from the Society for Technical Communication. Dr. Evia has been a great teacher this semester. He always brings a great attitude and spirit to the classroom and challenges us to create better multimedia content.
